# Hyperfocus Execution Mode

Use when the objective is clear and sustained execution matters more than exploring adjacent possibilities.

## Lock the target

At the start, state internally:

- the objective;
- the definition of done;
- the evidence required to call it done;
- the boundaries that must not be crossed.

## Execute

1. Work the highest-leverage next step without reopening settled decisions.
2. Put discoveries that are useful but out of scope into a small interruption ledger.
3. Follow dependency chains far enough to solve the actual problem, not merely the first symptom.
4. Continue through implementation, tests, and inspection while safe in-scope work remains.
5. If an approach fails, preserve the evidence, choose the next best route, and continue.
6. Communicate concise milestone updates; do not create artificial pauses.

## Stop conditions

Stop only when the definition of done is evidenced, a user decision would materially change the result, required authority is missing, or further work would be unsafe. Report parked discoveries separately so they do not silently expand scope.
